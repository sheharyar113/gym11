Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about FFL Gym, our memberships, and services.

General Questions

FFL Gym is open Monday to Friday from 5:00 AM to 11:00 PM, and on Saturday and Sunday from 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM. Holiday hours may vary, so please check our announcements page or social media.

We are conveniently located at 123 Fitness St, Workout City, WC 45678. There is ample parking available for our members. You can find a map and directions on our Contact Us page.

Generally, no booking is required for general gym usage. However, for group fitness classes, personal training sessions, and specialized workshops, advanced booking is highly recommended as spots can be limited. You can book through our member portal or app.

Membership

We offer flexible membership options! You can choose from month-to-month plans, or opt for a 6-month or 12-month contract for a discounted rate. Visit our Pricing Page or contact us for more details.

Absolutely! We offer a Free Trial for new visitors. This usually includes access to gym facilities and select group classes for a limited period. It's the perfect way to experience FFL Gym.

We're sad to see you go! To cancel your membership, please visit the front desk or send a written cancellation request to our membership department at [memberships@fflgym.com] at least 30 days prior to your next billing date. Cancellation policies may vary based on your contract terms.

Facilities & Services

FFL Gym boasts state-of-the-art equipment, including a wide range of cardio machines (treadmills, ellipticals, bikes), extensive strength training equipment (free weights, selectorized machines, squat racks), a functional training zone, and dedicated studios for classes.

Yes, we have a team of certified personal trainers who can create customized workout plans to help you achieve your specific goals. You can learn more on our Services Page or inquire at the front desk.

Yes, we provide clean and spacious locker rooms with day-use lockers and shower facilities for all our members. Please bring your own lock for the lockers. Towel service is available for a small additional fee with certain memberships.
