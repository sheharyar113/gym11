Membership FAQs

We offer flexible membership options! You can choose from month-to-month plans, or opt for a 6-month or 12-month contract for a discounted rate. Visit our front desk or contact us for more details on longer commitments.

Yes, we understand that life happens. You can freeze your membership for a minimum of one month and a maximum of three months per calendar year, for a nominal administrative fee. Please provide at least 14 days' notice.

We believe in transparent pricing. The prices listed are your monthly membership fees. There might be a one-time enrollment fee for new members, which is often waived during promotional periods. Any additional services like personal training or specialized workshops are charged separately.

Our Free Trial typically gives you access to the gym facilities and a selection of our standard group classes for a limited period (e.g., 3 days or 1 week). It's a great way to experience FFL Gym before committing. Specifics may vary based on current promotions.
